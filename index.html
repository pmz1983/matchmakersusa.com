The Method

Dating, by methodology.
Refined by practice.

Standing matters more than swipes. Intent matters more than attraction. The right tools matter more than the smartest algorithm.

The dating industry is a $9 billion market built around engagement — time spent, swipes logged, messages bounced. The longer you search, the more they earn. MatchMakers was built around a different assumption: the problem was never access to people. It was clarity about purpose.

So we built three systems and one methodology — refined over 7 years of human matchmaking, now applied through the Playbook and your Coach. The systems are designed to work together. Each reflects how matchmaking actually works when a person does it well — observation, response, and intent — not how an algorithm guesses at it.
