The Two Modes

Solo. Or together.

Solo Practice and Joint Practice are equal-validity modes. The Practice meets you wherever you are. The methodology holds across both.

The Five Capabilities

Continuity. Calibration. Conduct. Composure. Compounding.

Five capabilities that operate continuously across every Phase the methodology recursing. They are not features. They are the lenses the existing tools shift into when the work continues.

  1. I

    Continuity

    The relationship has a record.

    Memory. Timeline. Pattern history across years. The Practice carries forward what the Search began.

  2. II

    Calibration

    Alignment held over time.

    Intent does not freeze at commitment. It evolves with circumstances. Calibration is the ongoing discipline of holding alignment as both partners move through life.

  3. III

    Conduct

    Execution in real moments.

    Day-to-day frameworks. Scenario-specific scripts. Presence as a discipline. The methodology of how the relationship is conducted, moment to moment.

  4. IV

    Composure

    Discipline held in stress, not avoided.

    Friction comes. The framework offers a structured pause. The pattern is named. Both partners hold the choice. Composure is the Cartier discipline applied to the moments that test it.

  5. V

    Compounding

    The relationship deepens with use.

    Every engagement compounds the prior. Nothing resets. Long-term presence + ritual + shared experience deepen what the methodology has already built.

Courtship continues — commitment is the floor, not the ceiling.

Commitment renews — re-affirmed, not assumed.
