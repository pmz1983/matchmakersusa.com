What It Is

Not a chatbot. The Playbook's intelligence layer — active when you need it.

Every script in the Playbook is built on a behavioral principle. The Dating Coach knows every one of them. When you're in a real conversation and something is happening — a stall, a shift, a difficult moment — you don't have time to find the right page in a document. You need the answer now.

The Dating Coach is trained on the complete MatchMakers methodology: the 5-Signal Connection Code, the 5-phase framework, 50+ proven scripts, 9 Intent frameworks, and seven years of behavioral data from 66,000+ real member connections. It doesn't give you generic advice. It gives you the MatchMakers methodology applied to your specific situation.
