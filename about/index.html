Origin.

MatchMakers began with a question: why does dating, with all its tools and applications, leave so many people without the relationships they came for? More than a decade of matchmaking practice produced an answer — a framework that names the work and a Playbook that grounds the practice. The institution exists to scale that work to the millions of people who carry the weight of finding the right person.

How it works.

5-Phase × 5-Signal × 9-Intent framework, distilled across more than a decade of matchmaking practice into an 87-chapter Living Knowledge Graph called the Playbook. Every Coach turn, every Script, every Suitability Report, every Letter from Coach cites specific chapter chunks. Users can deep-dive into source. Trust is provable, not marketed.

The five phases.

Five phases describe the lifetime arc of a relationship's formation: Profile (where every member shows up in full), Connection (where both sides opt in), Courtship (time and intention, not race-to-conversation), Commitment (the outcome you came here for), Ongoing (live the outcome you've built). Every Coach turn names the phase. Every Suitability Report scores the position. Every Letter from Coach observes the work in context.

Calibration is the discipline. Refinement is the adaptation. Outcome is the constant.

The five signals.

Five signals diagnose where you are in the work: Intent. Position. Open. Build. Progress. The Coach reads them. The Playbook chapters teach them. The Suitability Report scores them. The signals are the diagnostic; the Playbook is the practice.

The nine intents.

Nine intent classes describe what you came for: Long-Term. Marriage. Fall in Love. Casual. Friendship. Companionship. Not Sure. Short-Term. Open to All. The Connection Code Engine begins with this calibration. The methodology meets you where you are.

What the Coach is.

The Coach is the methodology in conversation. Coach Lite is the choice tree at L1. Coach AI is the methodology applied at L2 Studio, citing the Playbook on every turn. The Coach speaks once, with deliberation. The Coach is held to the Brief. The Brief is held to the read.

AI provides structure and continuity. Human matchmakers introduce judgment, nuance, and accountability. The methodology authority anchors the work institutionally.

The business model.

Every engagement compounds. Nothing resets. The methodology runs the operating model. The Playbook grounds every output. The institution serves the millions who carry the weight of finding the right person. More than a decade in the making. Across years. Across tiers. Across every artifact and every interaction.
