About MatchMakers

The methodology behind the operating system.

MatchMakers is the institutional home of a methodology refined across more than a decade of matchmaking practice. The work is the methodology; the methodology is the work.

Where the methodology comes from ↓

Origin

The system exists. Most people just don't know it yet.

The methodology began as a single framework for the Search — observations about how connections form, how they hold, how they progress. Refined across more than a decade of matchmaking practice, it grew to span the work that comes before (the self) and the work that comes after (the partnership). The Playbook is the corpus. Three contexts. One system.

The work was not constructed; it was observed. Patterns surfaced across sustained matchmaking practice; the patterns were tested across many engagements; the patterns that held became the framework. The framework continues to be refined — not by replacement, but by deepening — as the practice produces material the prior framework had not yet recognized.

The framework is the methodology. The methodology is the work.
