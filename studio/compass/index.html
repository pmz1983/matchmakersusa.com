What the Compass does for you

The Compass is with you for any work you bring it.

The Connection Compass is with you for any work you bring it across Foundation (the work of self), Search (the work of finding a partner), and Practice (the work of holding what you find). Anything that lives in the methodology — questions about yourself, words you need to send, patterns in someone you’re considering, recurring feelings you can’t name yet, a reflection that won’t settle — the Compass meets you there.

It begins with a Reading: eight minutes of methodology-grounded questions. The methodology locates where your work is right now and builds a map across Foundation, Search, and Practice. That map is yours. The Compass remembers it across every session — three months from now, six months from now, the next time you reflect.

Type what you’re noticing — “I keep going back to his profile,” “I’m second-guessing this match,” “I’m not sure why I said yes” — and the methodology surfaces what it observes. It cites where it’s drawing from: your own Reading, your past reflections, the chapter in the Connection Code that speaks to your moment. You decide what to do with it.

Need to send a hard text? Decline a date? Say what you actually mean? Ask the Compass for a script. It calibrates the language to where you are — tighter, warmer, more direct, softer. You edit and send. The Compass holds the structure; you hold the voice.

Show it a profile, describe a date, paste a conversation. The Compass locates the pattern — never adjudicating, only naming what the methodology observes. The reading is yours to interpret. When the same word shows up in your reflections three times — overwhelm, uncertain, drift — the Compass surfaces it without you asking. This is appearing. Your Reading located you here. The Methodology Trail

Every output cites its source.

Every read the Compass surfaces carries a Methodology Trail chip — a clickable citation that opens the chapter passage that grounds the read. Your Reading. Your past reflections. The chapter in the Connection Code that speaks to your moment.

The trail is the audit. What frameworks the system used. What input it considered. What next step it observes. You can deep-dive into the source paragraph that grounds the read. This is what trust is, when trust is provable.
