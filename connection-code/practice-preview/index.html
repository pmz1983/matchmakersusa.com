The Five Phases Recursing

Intent. Profile. Connection. Courtship. Commitment.

The same five Phases that govern how connections form continue to govern how they hold. Practice is the methodology, recursing — deeper context; same architecture.

  1. I

    Intent recalibrates

    What kind of relationship are we sustaining together?

    Intent does not freeze at commitment. It evolves with circumstances. The Practice asks Intent again — and again, as life moves both partners through it.

  2. II

    Profile becomes joint

    The relationship has its own signals.

    Two profiles become a shared one. The relationship presents to the world as itself, not as either partner. Practice surfaces what the joint Profile is signaling — and what it should.

  3. III

    Connection deepens

    Presence over pursuit.

    The early-relationship Connection Signal was about establishing presence. The recursing Connection Signal is about deepening it. Practice surfaces the patterns of depth versus the patterns of drift.

  4. IV

    Courtship continues

    Commitment is the floor, not the ceiling.

    Most relationships stop courting after commitment. The methodology does not. Practice surfaces the rituals of ongoing courtship — the deliberate pursuit of the partner who is already chosen.

  5. V

    Commitment renews

    Re-affirmed, not assumed.

    Commitment is not a single moment; it's a cadence of moments where both partners renew. Practice surfaces the rhythm of renewal — the anniversaries, the rituals, the structural pauses.
