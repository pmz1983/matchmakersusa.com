The Methodology

A framework, not a feed.

MatchMakers is a relationship operating system, tailored to you. Not a dating app. This is an outcome system. The methodology is the institutional discipline behind every recommendation, every signal read, every refinement made — the through-line from your first Reading to the relationship you intend.

The methodology begins before the Search and continues through the Practice. It begins with the self.

The methodology is a five-phase framework, anchored to a five-signal architecture, calibrated against nine intent classes. It is not a personality test. It is not an algorithm that decides for you. It is a discipline — refined across more than a decade of matchmaking practice — that surfaces the questions worth answering and the patterns worth reading.

Five phases.

The methodology recognizes five phases. Each phase asks different questions, reads different signals, and calls for different work. The framework is the same. The work calibrates to the phase.

The Free Reading

What the Reading surfaces.

Seven to eight minutes. Roughly fourteen calibration questions, ledger-card by ledger-card. The framework reads the answers institutionally, not algorithmically. The result is a personalized Connection Code Brief — four pages, letterhead.

The Brief surfaces your dominant Intent class, your current Phase, and your initial Signal state. Twelve Playbook chapters open to the user, curated to the calibration. Coach Lite is the choice tree at the Connection Code; Coach AI is the methodology applied at the Studio purchase, citing the Playbook on every turn.
