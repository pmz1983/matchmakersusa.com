VIP Matchmaking

The methodology, applied with you and through you.

VIP Matchmaking is where the human matchmaker enters the work. VIP — Guided Execution: a methodology-trained matchmaker reviews, guides, and calibrates against your signals. Diamond — Bespoke Advisory: the Chief Advisor anchors the work institutionally at every signature moment.

VIP is consultation-first. The Chief Advisor decides the advisory layer that calibrates against your work. Letters are letterpress. Sessions are video. Audits are annual.

Eligibility

VIP Matchmaking is not for everyone.

That is the point. The most effective VIP engagements begin with people who already understand the methodology — who have applied it themselves, who know what they want, and who are ready for a human matchmaker to guide their execution. The consultation is how we determine whether that's you.

This is for you if —

  • You have applied the MatchMakers methodology and understand the five-phase framework.
  • You are clear on your Intent and serious about the outcome.
  • You want a named matchmaker, not an algorithm, reviewing your work.
  • You value discretion and expect a confidential, high-touch engagement.
  • You are ready to invest at the level serious outcomes require.

This is not for you if —

  • You are still exploring or undecided about what you want.
  • You have not yet engaged with the MatchMakers methodology.
  • You expect immediate results without process.
  • You are looking for an app experience or AI-only support.
  • You are not ready for a human matchmaker to hold you to the work.

The Process

From initial consultation to continuous advisory.

Every step in the VIP process exists for a reason. Nothing is ceremonial. The rigor of the intake is what makes the review meaningful. The quality of the coaching is what makes every connection worthwhile.

  1. 01

    Consultation · By appointment

    The Initial Consultation

    A private 45–60 minute conversation with the Chief Advisor. This is not a sales call. It is a structured assessment — of your goals, your Intent, your current situation, and whether VIP is the right service for where you are. Not every applicant is accepted. The consultation determines fit in both directions. No obligation to proceed.

  2. 02

    Intake · Accepted members only

    Deep Intake & Foundation

    Accepted members complete a comprehensive intake with their dedicated matchmaker. This goes well beyond what any profile captures. Your matchmaker learns how you present, what you project without realizing it, and what you genuinely need in a partner — distinct from what you believe you want. Intent anchors the foundation; the matchmaker goes deeper.

  3. 03

    Positioning · Before the work begins

    Profile & Position

    Your matchmaker reviews and optimizes your profile with the full methodology applied — photos, bio, prompts, Level strategy, Intent declaration. You are positioned before any conversation begins. The quality of your profile shapes the quality of the signals you broadcast and the signals you receive.

  4. 04

    Review · Framework calibration

    Suitor Review

    You source potential matches from your funnel; your matchmaker reviews each against Intent alignment, compatibility, and the full depth of your intake. Suitors are reviewed, not curated. Every reading is grounded in the methodology and over a decade of practice. You receive structured judgment with the specific reasoning behind each call.

  5. 05

    Coaching · Every connection, every phase

    Coaching Through Every Phase

    Once a connection moves forward, your matchmaker guides you through each phase the methodology recognizes — opening messages, escalation, the video call, the first in-person meeting. Preparation before each stage. Structured debrief after. You are never navigating a promising connection without the framework engaged.

  6. 06

    Ongoing · Until the outcome

    Continuous Advisory

    VIP engagement is calibrated by outcome, not by clock. Your matchmaker is with you for as long as the engagement requires. They adapt as situations adapt. If an approach isn't working, they diagnose. If it is, they build on it. The engagement continues until you have found what you came here for.
