The Process

From initial consultation to your first curated introduction.

Every step in the VIP process exists for a reason. Nothing is ceremonial. The rigor of the intake is what makes the curation meaningful. The quality of the coaching is what makes every introduction worthwhile.

01
Consultation · By Appointment
The Initial Consultation
A private, 45–60 minute conversation with a senior MatchMakers advisor. This is not a sales call. It is a structured assessment of your goals, your Intent, your current situation, and whether VIP MatchMaking is the right service for where you are. Not every applicant is accepted. The consultation is how we determine fit — in both directions. No obligation to proceed.
02
Intake · Accepted Members Only
Deep Intake — Profile, Intent, and Lifestyle Assessment
Accepted members complete a comprehensive intake with their dedicated MatchMaker. This goes well beyond the app profile. Your advisor learns how you present in person, what you project without realizing it, and what you genuinely need in a partner as distinct from what you believe you want. The Intent framework is the foundation. Your advisor goes much deeper.
03
Positioning · Before You're Presented
Profile Optimization and Platform Positioning
Your MatchMaker reviews and optimizes your profile with the full methodology applied — photos, bio, prompts, Level strategy, and Intent declaration. They prepare your presence in the platform before any introduction is made. You are positioned before you are presented. The quality of your profile determines the quality of the candidates who are shown to you.
04
Curation · Human Intelligence + Platform Data
Curated Match Selection
Your advisor identifies and personally vets potential introductions using Intent alignment, compatibility, and the full depth of your intake profile. Every candidate is assessed by a human before any introduction is made — not surfaced by an algorithm. You receive a curated selection with the specific reasoning behind each one. Every decision is grounded in the MatchMakers methodology and seven years of behavioral intelligence.
05
Coaching · Every Phase, Every Connection
Full 5-Phase Coaching Through Every Introduction
Once introductions are made, your MatchMaker coaches you through every phase of the methodology as it applies to each specific connection — opening messages, escalation, the video call, the first in-person meeting. Preparation before every stage. Structured debrief after. You are never navigating a promising connection without support.
06
Ongoing · Until the Outcome
Continuous Advisory — For as Long as It Takes
VIP MatchMaking is not a time-limited program. Your advisor is with you for as long as the engagement requires. They adapt as the situations do. If an approach isn't working, they diagnose and adjust. If something is working, they build on it. The engagement continues until you have found what you came here for.

Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers to the questions that matter.

Who is eligible for VIP MatchMaking?
VIP MatchMaking is available to MatchMakers members who have completed an initial consultation with a senior advisor and been accepted into the program. The consultation exists to ensure the service is the right fit. We will tell you directly if a lower tier is a more appropriate starting point — the goal is your outcome, not our revenue.
What does the initial consultation involve?
A private, 45–60 minute conversation with a senior MatchMakers advisor. Your advisor will want to understand your Intent, your current situation, what you've applied from the methodology, and what you're genuinely looking for. They will explain exactly how the VIP service works and what a realistic engagement looks like. No obligation to proceed.
How is pricing determined?
Pricing is set by consultation based on the scope of the engagement — the depth of the intake, the complexity of the curated match search, the level of coaching required through each phase, and the anticipated timeline. MatchMakers VIP does not operate on a fixed monthly retainer. The fee reflects the actual work involved in your specific engagement.
Is the Dating Coach required before starting?
VIP MatchMaking includes full access to both the Playbook and Dating Coach as part of your engagement. Clients who are already familiar with the 5-phase methodology get the most from every advisor session — but the VIP service is designed to bring you up to speed regardless of your starting point.
How is this different from the Dating Coach?
The Dating Coach is AI-powered — trained on the methodology and available 24/7. It provides responsive, knowledgeable guidance. VIP MatchMaking is a human advisory service — a named person who knows you, who curates introductions through judgment and direct knowledge of your situation, who coaches you through live situations with full context, and who is accountable to your outcome in a way no AI is. Both are valuable. They serve different needs at different levels of engagement.
What does 'not every application is accepted' mean?
The consultation is a genuine assessment, not a formality. We accept engagements where we believe we can deliver meaningful results within a realistic scope. If your situation isn't the right fit for VIP MatchMaking at this time — because of where you are in the methodology, your timeline, or the nature of your goals — we will tell you directly and recommend the most appropriate alternative. This standard protects both sides of the engagement.
How is confidentiality maintained?
Your engagement exists entirely outside the standard platform experience. Your intake, advisor sessions, and match selections are known only to your advisor. We do not share, discuss, or reference client engagements with anyone — including other members, other advisors, or internally outside your engagement team. This is the baseline standard, not a special accommodation.
